What is the Web Development Bootcamp?

The Web Development Bootcamp is an online platform designed to help students like you learn the essential
skills needed to become a successful web developer.
Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our courses are tailored to suit your learning needs.

Our Team

We have a dedicated team of instructors ready to guide you through your learning journey.
With three permanent teachers and one temporary teacher, you'll receive expert guidance and support every step of the way.

What you'll learn

In our bootcamp, you'll learn a wide range of skills, including:

  • HTML and CSS: Create beautiful and responsive websites.
  • JavaScript: Bring your websites to life with dynamic and interactive features.
  • Backend Development: Understand the basics of server-side programming and database management.
  • Deployment: Learn how to deploy your projects and share them with the world.

How It Works

  • Online Learning: All our courses are taught online, allowing you to learn from the comfort of your home.
  • Flexible Schedule: Time to learn is communicated via our WhatsApp group, so you can fit your studies around your busy schedule.
  • Bring a Friend: We encourage our students to bring new students who are interested in learning. Sharing knowledge is a key part of our community!
